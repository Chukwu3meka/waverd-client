# Cookie Policy

WaveRD uses cookies on its website to provide you with a better user experience. Cookies are small files that are stored on your computer's hard drive when you visit a website. They are used to store information such as name, handle, authentication details, etc., to reduce load time and to remember your browsing preferences for our site, such as theme.

---

## WaveRD uses cookies for the following purposes:

- To remember your login information so that you do not have to sign in every time you visit the website.
- To track your progress through the website so that you can easily find your way back to where you left off.
- To remember your preferences, such as your theme, preferred language, and font size.
- To collect anonymous data about how you use the website so that WaveRD can improve its services.

---

## Changes to Cookie Policy

WaveRD may change its cookie policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

---

## Social Provider

WaveRD uses social providers such as **Facebook**, **Twitter**, and **Google** to provide you with a better user experience. When you sign in to WaveRD using a social provider, we will collect your email address from that provider. We will use this email address to verify your identity and to update your account on WaveRD. We will not share your email address with any third party.

You can choose not to sign in to WaveRD using a social provider. If you do, you will need to create an account using your email address and password.
